## Lecture 1

* What is ecology?
    * *The scientific study of the interactions that determine the distribution and abundance of organisms* (Krebs 1972)
    * Organisms AND their biotic/abiotic environment
    * Aims to describe, explain and predict
* Why is ecology important?
    * Barriers to human sustainability are mostly ecological,  e.g. massive spike in $\Delta$CO2 in the past 300 years
